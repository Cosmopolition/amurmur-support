Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

🔒 All AI inference is completed on your device. Your voice recordings will never be sent to any server.

Article 1: Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Voice recordings and transcriptions (processed locally on your device only, not uploaded to our servers)

1.2 Information We Automatically Collect

iCloud sync data (managed by Apple, see Apple's Privacy Policy for details); Subscription status (processed through Apple IAP, we do not collect payment information)

1.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We explicitly do not collect: Device identifiers, location information, contacts, or any personal information beyond what is necessary for the app's functionality

Article 2: How We Use Your Information

Voice content: Used solely to generate titles, summaries, emotions, tags, and action items on your local device

iCloud data: Used for cross-device sync of your notes and organization results

Subscription information: Used to verify your subscription status

Article 3: Local AI Processing

Key Point

All AI inference is completed on your device. Your voice recordings will never be sent to any server.

This app uses an AI model (Qwen3.5-0.8B) running on your device to process voice content

All AI inference is completed entirely locally. Your recording content will not be sent to any server

The AI model itself does not have network connectivity and cannot transmit data

Article 4: iCloud Data

If you enable iCloud sync, your relevant data will be stored in your iCloud account

For users in mainland China, iCloud data is operated and managed by Guizhou-Apple (GCBD)

We cannot access your iCloud data

You can disable iCloud sync at any time in your system settings

Article 5: Third-Party Services

Apple iCloud: Data sync service, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

Apple App Store IAP: Subscription and purchase service, we do not store any payment information

Article 6: Data Security

Local data is stored with encryption

We do not create user accounts and do not store any personally identifiable information

Article 7: Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users aged 4 and above.

If we discover that children's information has been collected, it will be deleted immediately

Article 8: Your Rights

Delete data: Directly delete notes within the app, or uninstall the app

iCloud data: Manage through Apple ID settings

Article 9: Changes to This Privacy Policy

Significant changes will be notified through in-app notifications
