About Amumur

🎙️ Amumur is a voice memo app that uses on-device AI to help you record, transcribe, and organize your voice notes.

Core Features

  • Voice Recording - High-quality voice memo recording
  • AI Transcription - Automatic speech-to-text with intelligent punctuation
  • Smart Organization - AI auto-categorizes, generates titles and summaries
  • Tag Management - Organize all records with tags
  • Emotion Understanding - Recognize emotional state, provide natural responses and guiding questions
  • iCloud Sync - Seamlessly sync across your Apple devices

Privacy First

All AI processing happens locally on your device using the Qwen3.5-0.8B model. Your voice recordings are never sent to any server.

Troubleshooting

🔒 Privacy & Data

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes! All your voice recordings are processed locally on your device. We never see or store your audio files. If you use iCloud sync, your data is protected by Apple's encryption.

📱 iCloud Sync

Q: How do I enable iCloud sync?

A: Go to your device Settings → Your Name → iCloud → iCloud Drive, and make sure Amumur is enabled. Then enable sync in the app settings.

💰 Subscriptions & Refunds

Q: How do I manage my subscription?

A: Go to Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions on your device. For refunds, please contact Apple Support directly as all payments are processed through Apple.

🎤 Recording Quality

Q: How can I improve recording quality?

A: For best results, speak clearly and keep background noise to a minimum. The app works best when you're 20-50cm from the device microphone.
